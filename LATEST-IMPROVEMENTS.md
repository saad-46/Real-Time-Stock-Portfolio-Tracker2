# StockVault Premium Improvements Log

The following "Wow" features and premium enhancements have been added:

### 🚀 AI & Intelligence
- **Floating AI Assistant**: A fully integrated Groq-powered chatbot with voice and text support.
- **Natural Language Actions**: Execute trades like "Buy 10 Apple at $150" via voice or chat.
- **Smart Recommendation Engine**: Actionable insights (BUY/SELL/HOLD) with sector analysis.
- **Dependency-Free Logic**: Custom JSON parsing in `GroqAIService` for maximum reliability.

### 📊 Advanced Analytics
- **Technical Charting**: Stock charts now include **SMA (20)** and **EMA (10)** indicators.
- **Interactive Heatmap**: A visual "Heat Map" in Analytics representing portfolio performance intensity.
- **Risk Assessment**: Real-time **Risk Badges** (Low/Med/High) on stock cards based on volatility.
- **Dynamic Charting**: Support for TimeSeries charts with mouse-wheel zoom capabilities.

### 💸 Financial & Reporting
- **Multi-Currency Switcher**: Live conversion for INR, USD, EUR, and SAR in the top bar.
- **PDF-Style Statements**: "Print Portfolio Statement" feature generates a formatted financial statement.
- **News Sentiment**: Integrated news feed with Bullish/Bearish sentiment indicators.
- **Sector Filtering**: Advanced market filtering by sector and market cap.

### 🎨 UX & Aesthetics
- **FlatLaf Modernization**: Consistent dark-theme with premium rounded corners and smooth borders.
- **Market Pulse Ticker**: Real-time streaming ticker in the top bar for live market sentiment.
- **Notification Engine**: Native desktop alerts for trades, price targets, and system events.
- **Advanced Transactions**: New registry with real-time filtering and PDF export simulation.
- **Responsive UI**: Auto-adjusting layouts for different screen resolutions.
